Tgen Annual Robotics Showcase 2025

June 15-17, 2025
Paris Innovation Center
9:00 AM - 6:00 PM (Local Time)
Register Now View Schedule

The Tgen Annual Robotics Showcase is back and bigger than ever for 2025! This three-day event brings together robotics enthusiasts, industry professionals, and technology innovators from around the world for an unforgettable experience at the cutting edge of automation and AI.

This year's showcase will feature the public debut of our revolutionary T400 series, along with interactive demonstrations of our complete product lineup. Attendees will have the opportunity to experience our robots firsthand and speak with the engineers who designed them.

Highlights of the Event

The event will include technical workshops covering topics such as AI implementation, robot programming, and integration strategies. Industry experts will share insights on the future of automation and how businesses can leverage robotics technology to improve efficiency and productivity.

Networking sessions throughout the event will provide valuable opportunities to connect with peers, potential partners, and Tgen representatives. Don't miss the innovation panel on day two, where leading minds in robotics will discuss emerging trends and technologies.

This year we've expanded our exhibition floor to include demonstrations from partner companies and startups in the robotics ecosystem, giving attendees a comprehensive view of the current state of the industry and where it's headed.
